1K SINGLE PART EPOXY

OODA BOND 1K is a single-part heat cured epoxy adhesive with excellent adhesion to metal surfaces as well as composite materials. The high bond strength of this adhesive allows it to replace mechanical fastening, soldering, brazing or welding. OODA BOND 1K has been designed to be non-sagging, allowing the product to be used in large gaps and on vertical surfaces.

OODA BOND 1k is ideal for metals, ferrites, ceramics and composites and bonding of Magnets motor assembly and speakers assembly and electronic components as it has high wet strength and is non-stringing and produces an excellent drop profile as well as withstanding solder reflow processes.
